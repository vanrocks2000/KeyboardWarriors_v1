HOW TO PLAY
------------
There will be 3 rows of enemies approaching you
Move your player up and down using the up and down arrow keys to target the enemy you wish to attack
Type out the word given at the top of the enemy on the row you are standing
Press the enter key in order to attack the typed out word
Kill a certain amount of enemies to move on to the next level
If you make 3 consecutive typing errors, the enemy will speed up. To go back to the original speed, you will need to type 2 consecutive correct answers

Level 1
--------
Normal speed enemies with 1-2 words
No. to kill: 10

Level 2
--------
Faster speed enemies 1 character each
No. to kill: 20
